Bike Project

Lately I have gotten back into biking and am working on transforming my bike from a flat bar into a drop bar. Turns out this simple conversion requires the replacement of alot of components and it is often recommended to instead simply purchase a new bike. However, rather than get this discouraged this led me down a rabbit hole of buying lots of parts and researching bike geometry. One of the interesting things I discovered was that bikes use a quite anticuated sizing system that often leaves puts the avarage person in unrealistic positions that only really works for the build of professional cyclists and the sizing scale often leans too big.
